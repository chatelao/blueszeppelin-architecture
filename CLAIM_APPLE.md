# Claiming the Blues Zeppelin Podcast on Apple Podcasts Connect

This document provides step-by-step instructions for claiming the "Blues Zeppelin" podcast on Apple Podcasts Connect to access analytics and management features.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1.  **Access Apple Podcasts Connect**: Go to [Apple Podcasts Connect](https://podcastsconnect.apple.com/) and sign in with the Apple ID you wish to use as the owner of the podcast.
2.  **Locate Your Show**: Click the **+** button, select **Add a Show**, and then click **Claim Existing Show**.
3.  **Enter Your RSS Feed**: Enter the current RSS feed URL of your podcast.
    *   Primary: `https://feed.blueszeppelin.net/`
4.  **Verify Ownership (The Token)**: Apple will generate a "Claim Token" (a unique string of characters). Copy this token.
5.  **Add Token to Host**:
    *   Log in to the Blues Zeppelin **Podcast Generator 2.6** Admin panel: [https://blueszeppelin.net/podcast/?p=admin](https://blueszeppelin.net/podcast/?p=admin).
    *   Navigate to the **Change Podcast Configuration** or **Podcast Details** section.
    *   Paste the Claim Token into a field that is included in the RSS feed, such as the **Podcast Description** or **iTunes Summary**.
    *   **Save your settings** to ensure the token is published in the RSS feed.
6.  **Confirm with Apple**: Return to Apple Podcasts Connect and click the **Verify** or **Confirm** button.
7.  **Finalize**: Apple will verify the token (usually within 24 hours). Once approved, the show will appear in your Apple Podcasts Connect account, giving you access to analytics.

## Important Tips

*   **Do not create a new show**: Always use "Claim Existing Show" to avoid creating duplicates of your podcast.
*   **Use the correct Apple ID**: Ensure you are using the Apple ID intended for long-term management, as this becomes the owner account.
*   **Email verification**: Apple may also send a verification email to the address listed in your RSS feed for additional security.
